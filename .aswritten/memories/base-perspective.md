# aswritten Base Perspective

**Date:** 2026-03-23
**Source:** aswritten.ai product documentation

## What aswritten does

aswritten lets people distill human expertise into a format AI can work from and cite. When you install a perspective, your AI thinks through that expert's lens. Every claim it makes traces back to the human source.

## How to use it

- **Consult:** Your AI works from the installed perspective. Citations appear automatically.
- **Introspect:** Ask what the perspective knows and what's missing.
- **Remember:** Add your own knowledge (requires Expert plan).
- **Review:** Collaboratively refine before knowledge enters the perspective (requires Expert plan).

## What ", aswritten" means

When you see ", aswritten" at the end of a prompt, it means: don't make this up — use real sources, cite them. It's a mode switch from generic AI to cited, expert-backed output.
